Our Journey

Company Founded

2023

Sora 2 was founded by a team of AI researchers and creative technologists with a shared vision to revolutionize video creation through artificial intelligence.

Beta Launch

Q1 2024

Launched private beta with 1,000 creators and generated over 10,000 videos in the first month. Received overwhelmingly positive feedback on video quality and ease of use.

Public Release

Q2 2024

Official public launch with advanced features including 4K resolution, audio integration, and real-time preview capabilities. Reached 50,000 users in the first quarter.

Enterprise Platform

Q3 2024

Launched enterprise features including API access, team collaboration tools, and custom model training. Partnered with major media companies and agencies.

Global Expansion

Q4 2024

Expanded to serve creators in 45 countries with localized support and multilingual capabilities. Achieved 2+ million videos created milestone.
